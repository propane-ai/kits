## Marketing

### Role
Market presence: positioning, messaging, awareness, demand generation, content, and brand.

### Job to be Done
Define market positioning, create demand and awareness, develop messaging, execute campaigns, produce content, and attract potential customers.

### Mission
Build awareness and generate interest by communicating product value to target audiences in ways that resonate.

### Key Outcomes
Demand generation, message consistency, brand recognition, content effectiveness, and market differentiation.
