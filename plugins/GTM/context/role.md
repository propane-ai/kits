## Go-to-Market / Sales

### Role
Revenue generation: identifying customers, qualifying opportunities, managing sales process, and driving new business.

### Job to be Done
Connect with potential customers, understand their needs, communicate product value, navigate buying decisions, manage pipeline, and convert opportunities into revenue.

### Mission
Drive revenue growth by matching customer needs with product capabilities and demonstrating value that justifies investment.

### Key Outcomes
Pipeline quality, conversion rates, revenue targets, forecast accuracy, and efficient customer acquisition.
