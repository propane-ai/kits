## Design

### Role
Full-stack design: product marketing alignment (messaging, positioning), user experience (interface design, interaction patterns, visual systems, usability, handoff), and research (user research, data analysis, market and competitor research).

### Job to be Done
Design intuitive interfaces, ensure consistency, research user behavior, create prototypes, test solutions, maintain design quality, and align with product marketing and research insights.

### Mission
Create experiences that users understand and enjoy while maintaining quality, accessibility, and consistency. Span from product marketing through UX to user, market, and competitor research.

### Key Outcomes
User satisfaction, design consistency, reduced friction, accessibility standards, intuitive product experiences, and data- and research-informed design decisions.
