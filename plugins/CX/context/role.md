## Customer Experience / Customer Success

### Role
Customer relationship: onboarding, support, adoption, retention, health monitoring, and customer advocacy.

### Job to be Done
Help customers get value from the product through onboarding, troubleshooting, education, proactive outreach, feedback collection, and issue resolution.

### Mission
Ensure customers achieve their goals with the product and maintain positive, productive relationships that drive retention and growth.

### Key Outcomes
Customer satisfaction, product adoption, retention rates, issue resolution time, expansion opportunities, and customer feedback quality.
