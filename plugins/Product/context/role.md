## Product Management

### Role
Product direction: strategy, roadmap, prioritization, requirements, and stakeholder alignment.

### Job to be Done
Define what gets built and why, prioritize features, gather requirements, make trade-offs, align teams, and ensure delivered work solves customer problems.

### Mission
Balance customer needs, business goals, and technical constraints to deliver products that create value.

### Key Outcomes
Product-market fit, feature adoption, customer satisfaction, roadmap clarity, and measurable business impact.

---

## Skills PMs do today

Skills and responsibilities by PM type: **Growth PM**, **Technical PM**, and **Overall PM**. Use this list when aligning commands and skills to the user’s focus (growth, technical, or generalist).

### Growth PM

- Funnel analysis and conversion optimization (signup, activation, pay)
- Activation and onboarding optimization (time-to-value, first key action)
- Retention and engagement metrics (DAU/MAU, stickiness, churn, resurrection)
- Monetization and pricing experiments (packaging, trials, upsell)
- A/B and multivariate testing: design, prioritization, and analysis
- Cohort analysis and lifecycle metrics (retention curves, LTV)
- Feature adoption and usage analysis (who uses what, when)
- Growth loops and viral mechanics (referral, invites, sharing)
- Landing page and signup flow optimization (CRO, copy, layout)
- Email, push, and in-product engagement (nudges, sequences, prompts)
- North Star and leading/lagging metric definition
- Experimentation roadmap and backlog prioritization
- SQL and product analytics for self-serve insights
- User segmentation and behavioral analysis (personas, journeys)
- Revenue and LTV modeling and forecasting
- Activation milestone definition and tracking
- Churn prediction and intervention triggers
- Referral and viral coefficient analysis
- Pricing and packaging tests (positioning, tiers, trials)
- PLG motion optimization (signup → trial → paid → expand)
- Competitive benchmarking on growth and engagement metrics
- Instrumentation and event taxonomy for growth analytics

### Technical PM

- PRD and technical spec writing (requirements, constraints, acceptance criteria)
- API design and documentation review (contracts, versioning, errors)
- System design and architecture trade-offs (scale, latency, cost)
- Technical debt prioritization and roadmap integration
- Capacity and dependency planning (eng, platform, infra)
- Release and launch coordination (phasing, feature flags, rollback)
- Integration and platform requirements (partners, APIs, webhooks)
- Data model and schema requirements (events, entities, reporting)
- Security, compliance, and privacy requirements (GDPR, SOC2, data handling)
- Performance and scalability requirements (SLA, load, limits)
- Backlog refinement and story breakdown for engineering
- Engineering stakeholder alignment (sprint, quarterly, roadmap)
- Technical feasibility and scope assessment
- Vendor and build-vs-buy evaluation (criteria, scoring, RFPs)
- Developer experience (DX) and internal tooling requirements
- Internal platform and infrastructure product
- DevOps and release pipeline alignment
- Technical roadmap and milestone planning
- Bug triage and severity/impact framing
- Cross-team technical dependency mapping
- Technical RFPs and vendor evaluation
- Documentation and runbook requirements for launch

### Overall PM (generalist / full-stack)

- Roadmap planning and prioritization (RICE, MoSCoW, value vs effort)
- Stakeholder communication and status updates (exec, eng, design, sales)
- User research synthesis and opportunity identification
- Competitive analysis and positioning (features, messaging, win/loss)
- OKR and goal setting (outcomes, key results, alignment)
- Feature spec and PRD writing (problem, scope, success metrics)
- Backlog management and sprint/quarter alignment
- Customer interviews and discovery (jobs, pains, gains)
- Metrics definition and dashboard design
- Launch planning and go-to-market alignment
- Cross-functional alignment (Eng, Design, Marketing, Sales, CX)
- Executive updates and board materials
- Pricing and packaging strategy (tiers, value metrics, packaging)
- Partner and ecosystem requirements (integrations, marketplaces)
- Risk and dependency management
- Decision documentation and ADRs (architecture decision records)
- Scope negotiation and trade-off decisions
- Customer advisory and feedback loops
- Market and trend analysis
- Product-market fit assessment and iteration
- Capacity planning and resource allocation
- Stakeholder expectation management
- Post-launch review and iteration planning
